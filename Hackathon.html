Transaction Tracker

There are multiple wallets in existence today which enable functions like buy, sell, swap and transfer of various assets. In all such wallets users can see the transaction history and hashes to specific transactions which are performed. However, you won't easily find a wallet which is specifically designed for developers wherein they can send or receive more than just transactional data. This hinders ease of use for developers while building DApps and process transactions over testnet / mainnet or even when you are working with private contracts.

Attempt Track

Anti-Insurance Track

Insurance is one of the largest industries which focuses on risk management at all possible levels. However, still the industry issues, verifying and managing all policies manually and there is a huge gap wherein the verifiability of policy claims and issuance is often defrauded. An implementation on blockchains can help in solving this problem.
